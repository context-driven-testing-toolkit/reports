Vibium Cross-Channel Interest

Noah Sussman

Abstract

The Vibium community is growing at an exponential rate, showing clear growth in daily active users. The size of the community has doubled twice since the announcement of Vibium, and is on track to continue doubling at least twice more.

The daily pattern of Google searches for Vibium shows the classic chart pattern of a product that has many daily active users (DAUs) and is growing the number of DAUs on a daily basis.

The “bumps” in the chart represent people waking up every day to log on and talk about Vibium. That the bumps get larger over time means that more and more people are joining the discussion daily.

Reddit and LinkedIn Both Show Exponential Growth

r/vibium is growing exponentially

r/vibium has grown from 50 users to 248 users in four days.

So the size of r/vibium has doubled twice and is on track to double again within a week. At this rate there will be 1000 people in r/vibium and Jason will have to do an AMA, by September 15.

LinkedIn discussions are also growing exponentially

On LinkedIn there were 203 posts this week and 56 of them in the last 24 hours. In other words, 25% of all posts this week were written in the last 24 hours.
